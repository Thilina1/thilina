DECISION MAKING SUPPORTING PLATFORM FOR SMART AGRICULTURE (Agro Docror)

Agro Doctor, a transformative mobile app tailored for Sri Lankan farmers, agriculture experts, and students, harnesses advanced technologies, including Flutter, Firebase, and TensorFlow Lite, to combat the agricultural sector's decline. With plant diseases causing a staggering 35% annual crop loss in Sri Lanka, Agro Doctor's standout feature is its state-of-the-art plant disease detection. Leveraging machine learning and image processing, the app delivers early symptom alerts and treatment suggestions, providing farmers with crucial tools to protect their crops.
Beyond disease detection, Agro Doctor offers a comprehensive suite of agricultural tools, from sending alerts about crop diseases spread near the area to daily weather updates, fertilizer calculator, cultivation calendars, tips, market prices, and note-taking functionality. These services empower farmers with essential data, streamlining decision-making and enhancing overall productivity.
Moreover, Agro Doctor fosters community among farmers through an agricultural forum, promoting communication and knowledge exchange. Integrating machine learning, cloud computing, mobile technology, and image processing, Agro Doctor emerges as an accessible and affordable solution poised to uplift the lives of farming communities in Sri Lanka. By addressing critical issues and leveraging innovative technologies, Agro Doctor stands as a beacon of progress, promising a brighter future for Sri Lanka's agriculture.
